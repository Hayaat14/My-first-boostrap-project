Our Mission in Happy Tails.

At Happy Tails, our mission is simple, give every furry friend the love, care, and second chance they deserve. We work to create a safe, warm space where abandoned and rescued animals can heal, play, and find their forever homes. Every wag, purr, and nuzzle reminds us why we do what we do.

Our Story.

Happy Tails began as a small idea in a friend’s backyard, where we took in two stray dogs during a winter storm. Word spread quickly, and before we knew it, neighbors were dropping off food, blankets, and even more animals. What started with a couple of kennels has grown into a cozy haven for pets in need — and a big, happy family for us all.

Why We Care.

We believe every animal’s life matters. From the tiniest kitten to the oldest rescue dog, each one has a story worth cherishing. Seeing them transforming, from scared and unsure to happy and confident, is the greatest reward. At Happy Tails, we don’t just rescue animals, we restore hope.
